Our Story

Built on precision & pride

E&K Elite Lawn Care is a family-owned crew in Chilton County built around a simple belief: there's a real difference between a lawn that's been mowed and a lawn that's been cared for. We chase that difference on every property.

The "E&K" is us — and our name is on every yard we touch. That's why we sweat the stripes, the edges and the turf health most crews skip. From a clean weekly cut to a full season-long lawn health program, we treat your lawn like it's the best-looking one on the street, because we want it to be.

No rotating crews, no runaround. Just dependable, precise work from people who actually care how it turns out.
